Fun Facts

  • Estimated 1 million dogs in America have been named the primary beneficiary in their owner's will.

  • A cat will almost never meow at another cat. Cats use this sound for humans.
  • A fifteen year old cat has probably spent ten years of its life sleeping.
  • When teaching a puppy to come, begin by getting down to his level so he will not be encouraged to jump up on you.
